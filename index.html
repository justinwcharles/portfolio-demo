About

The intersection of art and technology is my jam

After 13 years of progressive experience in the HR industry, I am thrilled to transition to a new and exciting career path that better aligns with my personality and interests.

I've been passionate about the arts throughout my life, and I love learning about and building capability in photography, videography, and producing and DJing electronic music. I'm also an avid gamer - I'm fascinated when teams of talented artistic and technical minds work together to create one cohesive experience.

In this next chapter of my life, I'm focusing my passion for art and technology on solving UX/UI problems via human-centered design. I love simple, clean designs that get the user to their destination without friction and maybe even with some joy along the way.
